Pricing Strategy Built on Data, Not Assumptions

Pricing is one of the most powerful growth levers available to B2B companies.

Yet in many organizations pricing decisions are based on historical structures, competitor benchmarking, or internal assumptions rather than data.

I help companies build data-driven pricing strategies that increase revenue, improve margins, and support scalable growth.

Typical engagements include:

  • Pricing strategy and price structure design
  • SaaS pricing models and packaging
  • Pricing analytics and optimization
  • Revenue and margin improvement analysis

My background combines commercial ownership of pricing decisions with advanced analytics and revenue performance management.

The result: pricing decisions that are structured, data-supported, and commercially effective.
